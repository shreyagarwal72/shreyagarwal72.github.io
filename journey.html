My Life Journey

Mera journey ek simple bacche se lekar ek digital creator banne tak ka safar hai. Class 11 se maine apni creativity explore karni shuru ki – chahe wo Minecraft videos ho, Squid Game series, ya AI-generated music shorts. Maine seekha ki content creation sirf editing nahi hoti, ek emotion hota hai.

YouTube ke saath maine ek alag duniya jaani – jahan log apni kahaniyan, fun, struggle sab kuch share karte hain. Maine bhi wahi kiya – bina dare, bina rukhe.

Nextup Studio mera dream project hai jahan mai har emotion ko capture karta hoon – thrill, fun, heartbreak, ya revenge. Ab har short, har story, aur har sound ke peeche ek real connection hota hai.

Ye sirf journey nahi, ye meri pehchaan hai.
