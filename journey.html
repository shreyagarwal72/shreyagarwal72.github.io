My Life Journey 🚀

Main ek normal student tha jise editing ka shauk tha. Suruaat hui ek chhoti si YouTube video se jo maine Shotcut software se banayi thi. Dheere dheere editing aur storytelling ka passion grow karta gaya.

Pehle maine ek temporary gaming channel "Nextup Gaming" start kiya jisme maine Shorts upload kiye — Minecraft, funny moments aur Squid Game inspired videos. Jab maine dekha ki logon ko pasand aa raha hai, tab maine officially "Nextup Studio" launch kiya.

Nextup Studio ka vision simple tha — AI generated kahaniyan, music videos, Minecraft aur Squid Game shorts ka ek creative hub banana. Har video ke sath maine kuch naya try kiya, kabhi animation, kabhi story aur kabhi cinematic vibes.

Aaj bhi ye journey chal rahi hai — naye experiments ke sath, naye logon tak pahuchne ka mission leke. Agar aap naye ho toh explore karo aur channel ko support karo. ❤️
